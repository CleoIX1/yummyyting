My Story

Made with love,
from home

yummytingg_kitchen started as a quiet weekend ritual — ripe bananas, dark chocolate, the smell of something good in the oven. What began as baking for family turned into something I couldn't stop: a small home bakery built on care, honesty, and the belief that a good bake can make someone's day a little softer.

Everything I make is baked fresh, in small batches, and sent with a handwritten note. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just real ingredients and time.
